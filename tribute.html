Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar

14 April 1891 - 6 December 1956

Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was an Indian jurist,economist,Social reformer and political leader who headed the committee drafting the Constitution of India

BIOGRAPHY

  • April 1891:- Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar born at Mahu in Madhya Pradesh.
  • 1907 Married to nine year old Ramabai.
  • 1907:- Passed matriculation examination from Elphinstone High School, Bombay.
  • 1912:- Passed B. A. from Elphinstone College, Bombay.
  • 15th June 1913:- Leaves for the U.S.A. to join Columbia University, New York.
  • June 1915 Awarded M.A. degree in Economics.
  • June 1916 Awarded Doctorate in Philosophy degree.
  • 1926–1937:-
    1. Member of the Bombay Legislative Council
    2. "Chavadar Tale" Satryagraha at Mahad.
    3. Appointed lecturer at Government Law College, Bombay.
  • 1937–1942:-
    1. Published ” Thoughts on Pakstian ” Book. 20th Mar. 1942 Cripps & Dr. Ambedkar meet.
    2. Leader of the Opposition in the Bombay Legislative Assembly( Member of the Bombay Legislative Assembly)
  • 22 July 1942 – 20 October 1946:- Minister of Labour in Viceroy's Executive Council
  • 9 December 1946 – 24 January 1950:- Member of the Constituent Assembly of India Constituency
    • Bengal Province (1946–47)
    • Bombay Province (1947–50)
  • 29 August 1947 – 24 January 1950:- Chairman of the Constitution Drafting Committee
  • 15 August 1947 – 6 October 1951:- 1st Minister of Law and Justice
  • 3 April 1952 – 6 December 1956:- Member of Parliament of Rajya Sabha for Bombay State
